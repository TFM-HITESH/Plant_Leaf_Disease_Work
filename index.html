Plant Leaf Disease Classification

Welcome to the research guide for classifying plant leaf diseases. This page outlines the key steps in developing an AI-based solution for identifying plant diseases using leaf images.

Research Objective

Our goal is to support farmers by providing a fast and accurate tool for identifying plant diseases. This approach leverages machine learning techniques for classifying various leaf diseases based on image data.

Project Steps

1. Data Collection

Gather a diverse and extensive dataset of plant leaf images, covering various species and associated diseases. A well-rounded dataset is essential for training an effective model.

2. Segmentation (Optional)

In some cases, performing segmentation to isolate the leaf from its background can help the model focus on the relevant features of the leaf, improving classification accuracy.

3. Model Selection

Choose a suitable machine learning model for the task at hand. Depending on the complexity, you may opt for traditional methods or advanced deep learning techniques for image classification.

4. Model Training

Train the selected model on the collected dataset. The training process involves fine-tuning the model to recognize various patterns in leaf images that correspond to specific diseases.
